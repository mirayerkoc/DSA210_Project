‚ö†Ô∏è Limitations & Challenges

1. Geographic Scope

Analysis limited to single D-100 corridor location - may not generalize to entire Istanbul

2. Missing Weather Data

Critical limitation: Wind speed/direction, temperature, humidity not included

These factors are primary drivers of NO‚ÇÇ dispersion

Large residuals in ML models likely due to weather events

3. Temporal Coverage

2024 data only (single year) - long-term trends require multi-year analysis

4. Confounding Variables

Industrial emissions, construction activities, and other pollution sources not separately quantified

5. Class Imbalance

Rare AQI categories (<2% of data) difficult to predict - "Hazardous" class missing from test set
